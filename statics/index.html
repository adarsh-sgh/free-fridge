OUR PROGRAMS

A food bank operating alone can alleviate hunger in a small, yet mighty way. But a NGO connected to Free Fridge can make an immense impact by scaling faster and operating more efficiently. Free Fridge offers expertise, resources, and connections that give NGOs what they need to become leaders in their community for a hunger-free future..

Partnering with new Food Banks

Free Fridge will partner with other food banks to ensure no one goes to sleep on an empty stomach.

Assuring Saftey

We aim to preserve food safety in co-ordination with our resources to ensure traceability, ethical practices and operational effectiveness.

Building Capacity

We aim to accelerate the impact of our network by ensuring nutritious food distribution.
